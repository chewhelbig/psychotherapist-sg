What It Does

Clinical Notes and Reflection, Not Automation

FieldNotes works from your own narrative. You write a brief, informal account of the session — what stood out, what repeated, what felt difficult, what remained unfinished. FieldNotes restructures this material into professional clinical language, while preserving uncertainty, ambiguity, and relational complexity.

The tool does not infer meaning independently of your input. It works with what you bring, not instead of it.

Clinical Notes

Clean, professional session narratives and Gestalt-style SOAP notes grounded in your own account.

Reflective Prompts

Supervisor-style questions that open clinical thinking — patterns, repetitions, contact, and unfinished business.

Contact Cycle Overview

A Gestalt contact cycle mapping and process markers drawn from what you described in the session.

FieldNotes supports clinical thinking — it does not replace it.

All generated material remains your professional responsibility. FieldNotes does not diagnose, assess risk, or make treatment decisions.

Who It's For

Built for Reflective Practitioners

FieldNotes is designed for psychotherapists, counsellors, clinical psychologists, and mental health professionals engaged in reflective or supervisory practice. It is particularly suited to Gestalt-oriented, relational, humanistic, and integrative work — approaches that value process, contact, and field awareness.

FieldNotes is not for: clients, self-help use, or unqualified users. It does not diagnose, provide therapy, replace supervision, or act as a clinical authority.

How It Works

Four Steps, After the Session

  1. Sign in at fieldnotes.psychotherapist.sg with your email.
  2. Paste your session narrative — use a client label, avoid full names.
  3. Choose your output — Short or Full notes, with or without Reflection depth.
  4. Generate and download your clinical notes and reflective material.

Data & Responsibility

Confidentiality and Professional Use

FieldNotes operates in hosted, download-only mode. Session content is processed to generate output and is not stored as client records on the server. Your data stays under your control — saved locally on your computer, not stored in external databases, exportable and deletable at any time.

You are expected to anonymise all client material, comply with your professional and legal obligations, and use the app reflectively, not defensively. The app supports ethical practice. It does not remove responsibility from the clinician.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FieldNotes?
FieldNotes is a Gestalt-informed AI session companion for psychotherapists. It helps therapists turn brief, informal session narratives into structured clinical notes and supervision-oriented prompts, while leaving interpretation and responsibility entirely with the therapist.
Does FieldNotes diagnose clients or assess risk?
No. FieldNotes does not assign diagnoses, assess risk, or make treatment decisions. It does not evaluate outcomes or provide prescriptive recommendations.
Does FieldNotes store client data?
No. Session content is processed to generate output and is not stored as client records. Therapists are responsible for anonymising client information before use, and saving the generated material on their own computers.
Do I need technical skills to use FieldNotes?
No. If you can type your session notes, you can use FieldNotes. No coding or technical background is required.
